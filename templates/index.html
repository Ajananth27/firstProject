Covishield

Covishield is made up of weakened versionof a chimpanzee's common cold virus called
"adenovirus-ChAdox1"

Interval between 2 doses : "12-16 weeks"

Covaxin

Covaxin contain inactivated corona viruses, which can not infect a person but still can teach
the immune system to prepare a defense mechanism against the active virus.

Interval between 2 doses : "4-6 weeks"

Sputnik V

Sputnix v is 2 vector vaccine. This means that the vaccine uses two different kinds of adenovirus vectors,
"rAs26" for first dose & "rAd5" for second dose.

Interval between 2 doses : "21 days"
